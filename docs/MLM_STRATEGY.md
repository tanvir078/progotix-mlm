# Recommended MLM Strategy

## Best-fit model for this software

For this project, the strongest direction is:

- Product-first e-commerce MLM
- Direct sponsor commission on verified retail sales
- Small capped level bonus for leadership support
- Optional binary structure for team organization and team-volume matching

## Why this is the better path

- It gives the business a real retail engine instead of depending only on recruitment
- It supports repeat customer orders and product catalog growth
- It is easier to explain, audit, and report in software
- It avoids the highest-risk pattern: promising fixed daily returns on deposited money

## Do not build as the primary model

- Daily ROI or investment-return MLM
- Guaranteed daily profit from package buy-ins
- Downline income mainly tied to deposits instead of customer sales

## Software implication

The platform should treat these as the main money events:

1. Retail order paid
2. Package activated
3. Commission posted
4. Refund or reversal processed
5. Withdrawal requested and approved

## E-commerce-first commission flow

1. Customer places order
2. Order is paid
3. Retail commission becomes eligible
4. Team volume updates
5. Binary or leadership bonus is calculated from verified volume
6. Refunds reverse pending or posted commission based on policy

## Main product modules to prioritize

- Catalog and product pages
- Orders
- Customers
- Wallet and ledger
- Compensation engine
- Binary tree and team analytics
- Payout approvals
- Reporting and audit trail
